This Simulink file models 10 cars driving on a ring road. It helps understanding why traffic jams are created even when all cars are driving at a similar speed.

- ModelOV.mdl: Optimal Velocity model (Ref: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microscopic_traffic_flow_model)
- ModelG.mdl: Gipps model (Ref: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gipps%27_model)
